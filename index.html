Our company

IGL Granos S.A.

IGL

IGL Granos S.A. was established in 2005, initially dedicated to the brokerage of cereals and oilseeds. Starting in 2018, it began its activity as a processor and exporter of agricultural raw materials, focusing on organic products. Currently, the company focuses its attention on the provision of organic products, both grains and by-products, obtained from its fazon agreement with MBS Agroindustrias S.R.L. From 2020, its strategic alliance with InterContinental Foods LLC allows it fluid access to the North American market.
