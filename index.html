Our company

IGL Granos S.A.

IGL

IGL was established in 2005 as cereal and oilseed broker.
Throughout the years we began producing in our own ranches and further
processing our raw material thus transforming into edible vegetable oils and flours.
In 2018, along with our own production, we associated fellow ranchers and
decided to go Organic.
We began reaching international markets ensuring full traceability and full
guarantee on every product we exported.
Currently, the company focuses its activities on the provision of , pulses
and by-products such as vegetable oils and flours.
Our exclusive agreement with MBS Agroindustries, enables our full Organic
processing.
Our strategic partnership with InterContinental Foods in the US,
allows us a solid representation in the North American market.
